Pelican's Bar and Grill at the Sheraton Panama City Beach Golf & Spa Resort


August 18th 2020
Hit the beach and then head to Pelican's, a favorite gathering spot of both travelers and
Panama City Beach locals. This water-bound, open-air beach bar - with its tin roof and sprawling docks - welcomes a happy, hungry crowd ashore from their wave runners and pontoon boats, yachts, and sailboats. Come enjoy the relaxing atmosphere and soak in the sea breeze with the best sunset views in town. Open seasonally and situated at the end of the pier behind Sheraton Panama City Beach Golf & Spa Resort.
